Objective

Promotion and protection of interest of social work profession,Institutions and professionals in the state of Uttar Pradesh.
  1. To form a network and bring out the close contact among all the social work professionals /students/academicians/social work practitioner of Uttar Pradesh having degree of bachelorâ€™s in social work (UG) or masters in social work (PG) from any recognized department/institute/college/university of Uttar Pradesh.
  2. To undertake such activities that would promote acquisition of knowledge in the field of social work and other specialization under the social work degree.
  3. To publish papers, periodicals, journals and newsletters for the promotion of social work related specialization.
  4. To provide consultancy services to the industries, research institution, academic institution and other social welfare organization through its members and associate departments.
  5. To organize refresher courses, seminars, lecture/presentation, professional gathering and such other programs for the benefits of the social work professionals /students/academicians of Uttar Pradesh and public at large.
  6. To promote educational and research activities specially related to social work and its area.
  7. To facilitate the social work Institutions/departments to form their Alumni and organize their annual meeting.
  8. To help the social work institutions/departments to prepare and to design their course curriculum/syllabus as per the need of the job market and the real practice field.
  9. To promote the profession of social work across the State with the aim of improving the quality of service in the social welfare and social development sectors
  10. To act as State level organization and to represent social work professionals and Profession at national and international level.
  11. To improve the quality of social work education training and practice in the State and promote such activities as are conductive to social work profession and social work professionals.
  12. To undertake, organize and facilitate academic and professional interaction through studies, training courses, conferences, conventions, seminars, workshops, exhibitions, lectures and research in matters relating to social work education and profession.
  13. To develop professional code of conduct for professional social workers in the field of education, research, action and practice aimed at enhancing the status of social work profession and performance of the professionals.
  14. To work for creating better working conditions and improvements for work of social work professionals in general in the state and country.
  15. To co-operate and collaborate with any such organizations/institutions at regional/state/national or international level having similar objectives and similar international levels.
  16. To publish research studies, papers, books, encyclopaedia etc. directly or indirectly related to social work education and profession.
  17. To work for protection and promotion of the rights and interests of the members at different levels and initiate measures aimed at providing scholarships, awards, recognitions and improving working conditions and conditions of work professionals.
  18. To establish, open and run educational & technical institutions, training centres in the interest of the Association.
  19. To collaborate with government departments, public sector undertaking and private sector organizations to create employment, skill development, environment development, climate change, agricultural development, mother and child health, education, Corporate Social Responsibility activities/projects, water and sanitation, research & development, integrated community development initiate etc.
  20. To do any activity permissible under the law of land which may instrumental or incidental in promoting the aims and objectives of the organization.
  21. To co-operate and associate with similar organization working at State and national level.
  22. To acquire by purchase, lease, or otherwise, any property, rights, or privileges for the purposes of the Association.
  23. To encourage formation of chapters/branches at local and state level for promoting all or any of the objects of the Association.
  24. To suggest, study, offer opinion and advise on, or take part in, the framing of laws affecting the social work practice, social work education, social development policies and human care activities.
  25. To facilitate the recruitment process through campus interviews and out sourcing of social work professionals in both Government and non Government organization.
